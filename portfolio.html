COLLABORATIVE WORK

genGreen is a group project dedicated to showing individuals the truth about Climate Change using clear data sets. We focus on the effects of carbon dioxide emissions and displaying the issue at hand. Our website also provides resources for our users to reference on ways they can learn more and help fight this cause.

Food4All is a website focused on helping those who are affected and those looking to learn about food insecurity. We provide information on the topic as well as a take action page that encourages individuals to volunteer and donate. For those who do not have food accessibility, we implemented an interactive map to show local soup kitchens and food pantries.
